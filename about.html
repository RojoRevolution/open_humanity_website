Who are we?

Open Humanity is a platform designed and intended for humankind to spread hope, spread kindness, and spread love.

During times like these, it is important for us to remember that everyone is going through challenges - including the charities and organizations that aim to provide goods and services for those in need. And while dropping change in a bucket still helps, Open Humanity has a different approach to this “new normal” we as a planet are adjusting to.

In the middle of a global crisis that has affected and limited the way we handle most in-person interactions and operations, Open Humanity has the right idea: humanitarians and vendors can come together through a virtual and contactless marketplace to provide products and goods that allow charities and organizations to continue to function and thrive.

With Open Humanity, charities and organizations can create wish lists of products needed to fulfill their missions. Humanitarians can then purchase these products through Open Humanity and vendors will deliver them directly to the charities. It’s a chain of people helping people help people. It is exactly what the world needs right now.

At Open Humanity, we believe that we can restore faith in humanity by working together to change the world, by changing the way the world does charity. It just takes a little understanding and a desire to positively impact the lives of others.

If you are a charity in need, a vendor interested in teaming up, or simply a humanitarian looking to contribute, please join our platform and aid us in the fight to make the world a better, brighter, warmer place for us all.
